Getting Started


Step 1:

Download the Swoosh app and register with your mobile number.

Step 2:

Locate an available bicycle nearby using the map function in the app.

Step 3:

Activate Beginner’s mode if you’re new to e-bikes, to start off slowly while you get used to the accelerator and brakes.

Step 4:

Scan the QR code found on the handlebar or the side of the bicycle to unlock it.

Step 5:

Hop on the bicycle and push off with your foot a few times to get it started before you use the throttle.

Step 6:

End your ride in the app to make the bicycle available for someone else.

OUR VISION FOR THE FUTURE

Sustainability

Ways in which we are contributing to to the world.

climate-neutral mobility service

Our service has been climate neutral since January 2020 and we work to continually reduce residual emissions.

Promote sustainable adoption

By designing our service to be inclusive, safe and accessible, we a transition away from car centric mobility towards green and resilient transport.

Reducing car trips

Through encouraging people to travel with swoosh we aim to make you second think the next time you get in your car. Is a car trip necessary?

Measuring emissions

Committed to accountability and transparency, we were the first e-bicycle operator to conduct and publish a full Life Cycle Assessment of our service.

Reducing emissions

Our Climate Action Plan targets emission reduction focused on three key areas. Extending vehicle lifespan, zero emission operations and circularity

Offsetting emissions

Emissions we can not yet avoid and reduce through our core activities are offset, in order to reach climate neutrality.
